# Oat Milk

## Ingredients
- 100 g oats
- 750 g water
- 2 T maple/simple syrup
- pinch of salt
- 2 T oil

## Instructions 
1. blend and strain. Save soak for [Rye Sandwhich Bread](rye-sandwhich-bread)
1. To make yogurt: 8 min saute in instant pot while stirring. Cool and add 1T culture.
