# Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread

## Ingredients
Biga:
- 1 c bread flour
- 1/2 c water
- 1/4 t yeast

Soaker:
- 1 1/2 c whole wheat flour
- 1/4 c wheat bran
- 1 c water

Dough:
- 2 T sugar
- 2 t salt
- 1 T yeast
- 1/4 c oil

## Instructions 
1. Prep biga and soaker overnight
1. Mix dough and let rise
1. Form bread in loaf pan
1. Preheat 400° and bake at 350° for 45 minutes
