# Apple Pie

## Ingredients
- 3/4 c sugar
- 2 T flour
- 1 t lemon zest
- 1/4 t salt
- 1/2 t nutmeg
- 1/2 t cinnamon
- 1/4 t allspice
- 1 t lemon juice
- 5 lbs apples
- Top with egg white + 1 T sugar

## Instructions 
1. Bake at 425° for 25 min
1. Turn down to 375° and bake for an additional 30-35 min
