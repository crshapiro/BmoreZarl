# Buckwheat Pancakes

## Ingredients
- 1/2 c whole wheat flour
- 1/3 c buckwheat flour
- 1 T sugar
- 1/2 T baking owder
- 1/4 t cardamom or coriander
- 1/4 t salt
- 1 egg
- 2 c milk
- 1 1/2 T butter

## Instructions 
1. separate eggs. beat whites until stiff
1. Mix milk, melted butter, and yolks
1. mix wet into dry, then fold in egg whites
